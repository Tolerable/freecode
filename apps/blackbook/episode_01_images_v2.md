## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Image Duration Standards
- Title cards: 5-8 seconds
- Evidence/documents: 8-10 seconds
- Diagrams/charts: 10-12 seconds
- Text instruction: 7-8 seconds
- Closing cards: 8-10 seconds with fade

### Animation Guidelines
**Minimal motion only**:
- Slow zoom on documents (Ken Burns effect)
- Fade in/fade out transitions
- NO dramatic transitions
- NO fast cuts
- NO "spooky" effects

### Typography Standards
- Clean sans-serif for modern content
- Typewriter monospace for classified documents
- High contrast for readability
- No decorative fonts
- Accessibility-friendly sizing

### Color Palette
**Primary**: Black, white, dark gray
**Accents**:
- Red (Monster Lies)
- Blue (Asylum)
- Orange (Case Files)
- Violet (Cult Files)
- Gold (Federal Files)

**Avoid**: Saturated colors, neon, "horror movie" aesthetics

### Overall Visual Strategy
Think: **Educational documentary**, not horror entertainment
- Ken Burns documentaries
- True crime investigation boards
- Academic presentations
- Government briefing materials
- Field manuals
- Forensic photography

**NOT**: Movie posters, music videos, dramatic scenes, illustrated fantasy art

---

## ACCESSIBILITY NOTES

- All text large enough to read at 1080p
- High contrast for visual impairment accommodation
- No flashing or strobing (epilepsy safety)
- Images support narration, never contradict
- Alt text for each image in video description
