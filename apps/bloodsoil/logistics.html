Turning Beds Into Coffins

Supply Chain
The industry words are inputs and amendments. Their words are tether and cover. The same pallets that move calcium nitrate and coco coir also move pinches of homeland soil in sealed sachets, wake pads pre-soaked, and drums of "tea" with no paperwork. The result is a sleeper who never feels far from the old house.
