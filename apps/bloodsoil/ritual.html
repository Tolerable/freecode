The Ritual

Steep — not boil

The oath binds soil to blood so the tether carries orders clean. The timing is everything; the chemistry is simple; the mistakes are loud.

  • Prick & Pinch: one sire-drop + homeland pinch into blood/bone tea.
  • Steep 13 minutes (no boil) — keep the volatile carriers alive.
  • Twilight feed: fans off 3 minutes; let aerosols settle into trays and pads.
  • Wake Pad: same blend under the sleeper; swap after each harvest or rain event.
  • Boil freaks — rush job cooks carriers; glamour lags; frenzy spikes.
  • Pad rot — “sleep-salt” builds; paralysis on wake then violent rebound.
  • Wrong pinch — tether tries to sync to another bed; orders misfire mid-hunt.
  • Swap the pinch for sterile fines → homesick drift.
  • Salt pads with iron filings → micro-shocks that break timing.
  • Policy-mandate “boil” on any seized tea → their crews deliver you sluggish predators.
