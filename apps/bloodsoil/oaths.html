The Root Oath & Wake Pads

Procedure book, redacted
The chemistry must match the memory. The oath binds soil to blood so the rhizosphere carries orders without slur. Failure yields predators that over-feed, under-think, and draw hunters.
  • Mix homeland pinch + single sire-drop into blood/bone tea; steep 13 minutes; no boil.
  • First watering at civil twilight; fans off for 3 minutes to let vapors settle.
  • Match a Wake Pad beneath the sleeper; refresh after each harvest.
  • Impatient boil — knocks out volatile carriers; tether desync.
  • Pad rot — sleep-salt buildup causes freeze then frenzy.
  • Foreign pinch — tether re-homes to donor bed across town.
  • Swap the pinch; sterilized sand causes homesick drift in the glamour.
  • Seed Wake Pads with iron filings (salt-shock); throws off timing.
  • Document the boil; sloppy crews write your affidavit for you.
If the soil remembers a dead king, do not sleep near it.
