Cut the noise

Street lore mixes truth with flex. Here’s the cut you can actually use in rooms and reports.

  • “Any blood in soil breeds vampires.” — No. You need a homeland key.
  • “Garlic in pots blocks them.” — It only masks tells; it doesn’t break the tether.
  • “Burn the garden.” — Overkill. Community pays; signature survives elsewhere.
  • First brew at twilight matters. Fans off window is real.
  • Wake Pads mirror the beds; swap them and you’ll see timing collapse.
  • Clones can ferry signatures indirectly. Routes matter.
  • Don’t solo. Document airflow, timing, and what handlers actually do.
  • Gloves on tins and liners. They carry more story than you want on your skin.
  • Loop in garden stewards; you need the neighborhood, not a headline.
