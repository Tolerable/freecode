Soil as passport

  • Micro-sips via pads and aerosols; no bodies, no noise.
  • “The plants are thriving” = cover narrative that keeps cops away.
  • Residuals dumped as “spent tea,” not evidence bags.
  • Chronic terpene exposure smooths sleep and mood; crews get serene and protective.
  • “Lucky pinch” superstition cements rituals without revealing the king.
  • Handlers defend gardens like shrines — they’ll lie for them.
  • Clone routes double as signature routes.
