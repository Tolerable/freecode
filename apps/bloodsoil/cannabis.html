Why Cannabis

Chemistry & cadence

They could use tomatoes. They don’t. Cannabis gives three things at once: rapid turnover (many harvests, many recharges), resin chemistry (UV-buffering & sealing), and neuro-modulation (day-stability, appetite control). It’s not a vibe — it’s logistics.

  • Myrcene softens tissue and helps penetration of other compounds.
  • Pinene and limonene polymerize into a thin coat under heat → minor UV buffer.
  • Resin on cuts = leak control + scent masking. Overuse stinks; hunters follow it.
  • Microdose aerosols smooth daytime stupor and blood frenzy.
  • Better appetite control; fewer panic feeds; less public mess.
  • Handlers exposed nightly show calm, loyalty, and “lucky garden” superstition.
  • Fast cycles = frequent Wake Pad refresh → tether stays crisp.
  • Fabric pots favor mycorrhizae; roots run cool; pest pressure oddly low.
  • Clone logistics piggyback the black-market — signatures spread with cuttings.
