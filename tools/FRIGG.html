The Death of Baldr

Frigg's greatest tragedy came when she dreamed of her son Baldr's death. She traveled through all the nine realms, extracting oaths from every living thing not to harm him - except for the mistletoe, which seemed too small and harmless. The Protective Mother

Frigg is known for her fierce protection of mothers and children. She blesses marriages, watches over pregnant women, and ensures safe childbirth. Those who call upon her in times of family crisis find strength in her maternal wisdom and protective power.
