Healthy Partner Traits

In a healthy relationship, your partner is emotionally stable, supportive, reliable, and treats you as an equal partner in life's challenges.

Family Emergency Response

A supportive partner asks "What can I do to help?" and takes care of home responsibilities so you can focus on the family crisis.

Daily Household Responsibilities

Household tasks are shared based on ability and availability. Both partners contribute without being asked.

When You're Stressed
